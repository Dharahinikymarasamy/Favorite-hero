Sivakarthikeyan


Hits of SK

Sivakarthikeyan (born 17 February 1985), known professionally as SK, is an Indian actor, playback singer, and producer known for his work in Tamil cinema. He began his career as a television anchor and made his film debut with Marina (2012). He rose to fame with films like Kedi Billa Killadi Ranga (2013), Varuthapadatha Valibar Sangam (2013), Maan Karate (2014), and Rajini Murugan (2016). He later starred in Remo (2016), Velaikkaran (2017), and Seema Raja (2018). Known for mixing comedy with social themes, SK gained further acclaim for Doctor (2021), Don (2022), Prince (2022), and Ayalaan (2024). He also runs his own production house, Sivakarthikeyan Productions, and is one of the most bankable stars in Tamil cinema today.
